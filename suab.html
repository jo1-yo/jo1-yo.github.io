Why we exist

Our Mission

Everyone deserves to grow up in a safe and respectful environment. However, I personally experienced the harm and loneliness caused by bullying. Though painful, that experience became the driving force behind founding the Student Union Against Bullying (SUAB).

When we choose to stand up, it's not just for ourselves, but for those who continue to suffer in silence.

Bullying is not just a personal issue; it's a social problem. Through SUAB, I hope to create a platform where every voice that has been hurt can be heard, and every person who needs help can receive support.

About SUAB

SUAB operates with two branches, each with its own focus but united in our mission against bullying.

In action

Our Achievements

Charity Sales

Organized charity sales at various schools in Beijing, successfully raising over 20,000 yuan in donations to support anti-bullying initiatives and victim assistance programs.

Workshops & Drama

Conducted interactive workshops and drama performances on campuses to educate students about bullying prevention and create empathy through storytelling and role-playing.

Pink Day Events

Organized Pink Day events with pink-themed decorations to raise awareness about bullying, creating a visual symbol of solidarity and promoting a culture of kindness on campus.

Digital Education Platform

As the president, I developed an anti-bullying website featuring my digital avatar to guide visitors through anti-bullying pamphlets and resources. Visit our educational platform.

Vassar Anti-Bullying Week

Vassar Anti-Bullying Week was a campus-wide initiative sponsored by the Engaged Pluralism Office, engaging 300+ participants, with 100+ community members contributing messages of support and kindness on the Empathy Wall.

Paint for Care

Paint for Care was a two-hour open painting event with free snacks, where students freely expressed themselves around anti-bullying themes while unwinding during finals week.
