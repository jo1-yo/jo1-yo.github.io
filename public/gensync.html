Gensync

An AI-powered platform that brings seniors and young learners together for real conversation and shared learning. Data from the World Health Organization shows that around 25% of older adults globally face social isolation. A 2024 national survey also found that 33% of older adults often feel lonely.

→ View Code

Our Match page uses a smart algorithm inspired by platforms like Tinder to help connect users across generations. When a young user signs up and indicates an interest—such as playing Go—we recommend older users who share the same passion. The same goes for seniors: if they've expressed a hobby or preference, the system will introduce them to like-minded young people. It's more than just matching—it's about sparking conversations and building friendships based on shared interests.

Our platform features a powerful chat function powered by GPT-4, designed to bridge the communication gap between generations. Acting as a real-time "interpreter," our AI ensures that conversations between young people and older adults are smooth, natural, and mutually understandable. Whether it's explaining modern slang or clarifying traditional expressions, our chat feature translates language in a way that fosters empathy, connection, and meaningful exchange across generations.
