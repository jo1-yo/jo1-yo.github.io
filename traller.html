Traller

AI-Powered People Search and Profiling Agent

Project Overview

Traller is an AI-powered people search and profiling agent. Unlike traditional search engines, Traller transforms scattered, unreliable information into a clear, interactive knowledge graph. With just a name, a phrase, or even a vague description, it maps a person's digital footprint, highlights key events, and uncovers meaningful connections.

Originally built at a hackathon, Traller was awarded Second Prize in the AdventureX Tencent Cloud Track, earning a ¥5,000 prize for its innovation in combining language models with multi-source data for next-generation intelligence tools.
